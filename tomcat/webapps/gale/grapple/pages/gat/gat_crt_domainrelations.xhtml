The Domain Relations Tab in the Editor

The Domain Relations Tab of the PRT Editor is shown below:

In this tab you can assign this PRT to some domain relations. This means that when the PRT is used in a (without the need to assign any concepts to sockets) the PRT will automatically be applied to all instances of a relation. For instance, one could automatically associate a "knowledge propagation" PRT with all instances of a "parent" DM relation to have knowledge propagated throughout the concept hierarchy of a course. Unfortunately the CAM tool does not currently support this, so the Domain Relations tab has no effect.
