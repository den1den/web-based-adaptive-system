Create Concepts

Creation of conceptual structure is the most fundamental part of designing an adaptive hypermedia application.

The creation of a conceptual structure involves the following three steps.

  1. Design the conceptual structure of your application.
  2. Create the concepts used by the AHA! system.
  3. Draw the various concept relationships between the defined relations.
